# 📈 Stock Portfolio Tracker

A self-contained, single-file stock portfolio tracker that runs entirely in your browser. No signup, no backend, no data leaves your computer. Just download one HTML file and open it.

**[⬇ Download the latest version](../../releases/latest)**

---

## What it does

This is a **personal calculator** for tracking your stock holdings with broker-accurate fee calculations. It's built for people who want to know their *real* profit and loss after commissions, taxes, and currency conversion — not just the headline number.

- **Break-even tracking** — see exactly what price you need to sell at to break even, including buy and sell commissions
- **Multi-currency** — track holdings across 14 currencies (USD, SGD, HKD, EUR, GBP, JPY, and more) with live FX conversion to your chosen reporting currency
- **Broker fee presets** — 68 broker configurations across 8 countries (🇸🇬 🇺🇸 🇭🇰 🇦🇺 🇬🇧 🇲🇾 🇮🇳 🇯🇵), or set up your own custom fee structure
- **What-if calculators** — simulate selling or buying more shares before you act
- **Live prices** — connect a free Finnhub or Alpha Vantage API key, or enter prices manually
- **Monitoring list** — track stocks you're watching but haven't bought yet

## How to use it

1. **Download** the HTML file from the [Releases page](../../releases/latest)
2. **Open it** in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) — just double-click the file
3. **Set up your broker** — pick from the presets or enter custom fees
4. **Add a price source** — get a free API key from [finnhub.io](https://finnhub.io) (30 seconds, no credit card), or use manual price entry
5. **Add your holdings** — manually or by importing a CSV

Your data is saved inside the HTML file itself when you click "Save HTML." Nothing is ever uploaded anywhere.

## Privacy

This tool runs **100% in your browser**. There is no server, no account, no tracking, no analytics. Your portfolio data, API keys, and broker settings stay on your device. The only external calls are to your chosen price provider (Finnhub or Alpha Vantage) to fetch live stock prices — and only if you choose to use live prices.
